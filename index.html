About Boracay

Boracay is a small island in the Philippines located approximately 315 km (196 mi) south of Manila and 2 km off the northwest tip of Panay Island in the Western Visayas region of the Philippines.


Famous for its stunning White Beach, which stretches over 4 kilometers of powdery white sand and crystal-clear turquoise waters, Boracay has consistently been named one of the best islands in the world by numerous travel publications.


Beyond its famous beach, Boracay offers a vibrant nightlife, diverse water activities, and a welcoming atmosphere that keeps visitors coming back year after year.

Top Attractions

White Beach

The main beach of Boracay, known for its powdery white sand and stunning sunsets. Divided into Station 1, 2, and 3, each with its own unique vibe.

Puka Shell Beach

A quieter alternative to White Beach, named after the puka shells that are abundant here. Perfect for those seeking a more peaceful beach experience.

Diniwid Beach

A small, secluded beach just north of White Beach. Accessible by a short walk or boat ride, it offers a more intimate setting with beautiful rock formations.

Popular Activities

Island Hopping

Explore the surrounding islands and hidden lagoons on a traditional Filipino boat.

Kiteboarding

Take advantage of Boracay's consistent winds at Bulabog Beach, a world-renowned kiteboarding spot.

Snorkeling & Diving

Discover vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life in the crystal-clear waters around Boracay.

Beach Relaxation

Simply unwind on the famous white sand beaches and enjoy the spectacular sunsets.

Travel Information

How to Get There

Fly to either Caticlan Airport (closer) or Kalibo Airport (further). From there, take a boat to Boracay Island.

Best Time to Visit

The dry season from November to April offers the best weather with less rainfall and calmer seas.

Local Cuisine

Don't miss trying fresh seafood, Filipino classics like adobo, and the famous mango shakes.

Accommodation

Options range from luxury resorts to budget-friendly hostels, with most located along White Beach.
